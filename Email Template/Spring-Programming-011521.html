Spring 2021 Professional Development at CTLT
The Center for Teaching, Learning, and Technology offers a wide-range of professional development opportunities this semester to support how you teach and how your students learn. Once again, we're partnering with talented faculty and staff from across campus to explore topics ranging from online course design and delivery to effective communication with students regardless of modality.

This semester's events feature both synchronous and asynchronous learning opportunities. Visit CTLT.IllinoisState.edu/Spring for full descriptions and registration information. CTLT events are free and open to all faculty, staff, and graduate students at Illinois State.

If you need a special accommodation to fully participate in a CTLT event, please email CTLT@ilstu.edu.
